Typing Speed

Test your typing speed with cool quotes! The engine will keep running until time runs out, or your most recent input matches the quote's last character, at the right spot. (for the tech-ers, it's matching array index values)


wpm: adjusts your speed to account for accuracy.
(your average words / time taken) - mistakes, normalized to minutes

raw wpm: your true speed
(your average words) / time taken, normalized to minutes

accuracy:
total correct characters / total characters
